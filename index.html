I fell in love with art and design in high school. I attended advanced programs at Moore College of Art and Design and at Perkins School of Art. After high school I studied Digital Media and Studio Art at Albright College. Following college I continued on to graduate school in Florence, Italy where I studied Graphic Design and Visual Communications at Accademia Italiana, the renowned art college. For the past 10 years I have been freelancing for small businesses and nonprofits and working as a Graphic Designer / SEO Specialist.
