About me



I have always loved designing in the digital space. My favorite projects to work on are websites, apps, ads, and email designs. I am passionate about creating content that is pleasing to the eyes and easy to understand for users. Over the past five years, I have transitioned from being a Graphic Designer to a more thoughtful UX / UI Designer who puts data and the user first. My interests in UX / UI are most closely focused on the accessibility and the equity of projects.
